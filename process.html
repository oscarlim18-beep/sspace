Our Process

From first discussion to final handover, Square Space keeps every stage structured, clear, and professionally managed.

How We Work

Our process is designed to move each project from idea to completion with better clarity, stronger coordination, and more consistent execution. We focus on communication, practical planning, CIDB-aligned standards, and disciplined delivery at every stage.

01

Consultation

We begin by understanding your project requirements, business goals, site objectives, and preferred direction. This allows us to identify the right starting point and align expectations clearly from the beginning.

02

Site Visit & Assessment

Our team reviews site conditions, measurements, and practical considerations to ensure the proposed solution is realistic, efficient, and aligned with the actual environment.

03

Proposal & Planning

We prepare the proposed scope, planning direction, estimated requirements, and workflow approach so the client has a clear understanding of the next steps before execution begins.

04

Design Development

Concepts are refined into practical layouts, material selections, and project-ready design outputs that support both functionality and brand presentation.

05

Execution & Coordination

Once approved, we proceed with implementation, sourcing coordination, and on-site management to keep the project moving forward in a structured and well-managed way.

06

Handover & Support

After completion, we review the final outcome, complete handover, and provide the necessary support to ensure the space is ready for use with confidence.

What Clients Can Expect

Our process is built to create a smoother, more dependable experience from start to finish.

Clear Communication

Regular updates and more transparent discussion throughout the project journey.

Structured Workflow

A systemized approach that reduces confusion and supports better decision-making.

CIDB-Aligned Standards

Work delivered with attention to professional standards, quality, and accountability.

Reliable Coordination

Execution supported by careful planning, sourcing coordination, and project control.
