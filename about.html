About Us

Learn more about Square Space, our values, and our commitment to delivering CIDB-certified design and build solutions for developer and retail environments.

Who We Are

Square Space is a design and build company focused on delivering functional, value-driven spaces for developers, retail environments, and business-focused projects.

We believe every space should support performance, brand identity, and long-term business growth. That is why we combine planning, coordination, certified standards, and quality execution to create spaces that work in real-world environments.

From concept development to project completion, our team is committed to providing dependable solutions that align with our clients’ goals, operational needs, and project expectations.

Our Journey

Our journey reflects how Square Space has grown through trust, quality, and a commitment to delivering long-term value across every project.

2022
Establishment

Square Space was established with a clear vision to deliver reliable and value-driven solutions for modern business spaces.

2023
Value-Driven Furniture Solutions

Expanded into providing value-driven furniture solutions across retail, developer, and office environments, strengthening our foundation in functional and practical space delivery.

2024
End-to-End Design & Build Expansion

With the trust of our clients, Square Space expanded into full end-to-end design and build services and grew its presence across Southeast Asia, including Singapore and the Philippines.

2025
CIDB Certification & Standards

Strengthened our capabilities through CIDB certification, reinforcing our commitment to recognized construction standards, quality assurance, and reliable project delivery.

2026
Operational Strength & Systemization

Continuing to grow as a stronger organization, Square Space is enhancing internal IT systems and workflows to achieve more structured operations, improved efficiency, and consistent project delivery.
